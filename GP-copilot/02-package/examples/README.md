## Case Study: SaaS Company Policy Rollout

**Client:** CloudApp (SaaS platform, 30 engineers)

**Challenge:** Pass SOC 2 audit, no policy enforcement in place

**Timeline:** 6 weeks from zero to full enforcement

**Outcome:**
- 387 violations → 0 violations
- SOC 2 Type II passed
- Zero blocked deployments (after Week 3)
- 90% developer self-fix rate
